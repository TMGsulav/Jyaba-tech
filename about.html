Our Story

Jyaba Devs started as a small group of friends in the technology industry who met through local meetups in Pokhara. After realizing the broad appeal and clear need for a developer community in the city, the group publicly launched with its first event in 2024.

Since its inception, Jyaba Devs has helped its members acquire new skills and find success in the technology industry. Today, with more than 500 active members and a growing attendance at our hosted events, we are proud to serve as a thriving hub for Pokhara's software developer community.

Our partnerships with local colleges, tech incubators, and startups mean there's something for everyone at Jyaba Devs — whether you're a developer, student, technologist, or entrepreneur.

What We Stand For

Our values guide everything we do, from the events we host to the partnerships we form.

Community First

We believe in the power of community. Our events bring together developers of all skill levels to learn, share, and grow together in Pokhara.

Accessible to All

Every event is free and open to everyone. We're committed to breaking down barriers and making tech education accessible across Nepal.

Quality Content

From workshops to talks, we focus on delivering high-quality, practical content that helps developers succeed in their careers.
