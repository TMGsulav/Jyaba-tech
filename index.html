Our Values

What drives the Jyaba Devs community in Pokhara

Community First

We believe in the power of community. Every event, workshop, and resource is designed to bring Pokhara's developers together to learn, share, and grow.

Accessible to All

All of our events and resources are completely free. We strive to remove barriers to entry and make tech education accessible to everyone in Pokhara.

Quality Content

From hands-on workshops to expert talks, we curate high-quality content that helps developers at every stage of their journey.
