Bridging the Digital Divide

Students and educators at public schools and universities in Pokhara often face budget constraints, lengthy procurement processes, and limited access to the computing resources they need. The Cloud Project removes those barriers entirely, giving students and organizations immediate, free access to run virtual machines, test code, and work on real infrastructure projects.

Our mission is to ensure that every aspiring developer in Pokhara has the tools they need to learn, build, and grow — regardless of their economic background.
